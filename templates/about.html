About JigyaSci

Bridging the gap between academic research and industry innovation through intelligent collaboration

Pioneering India's Innovation Ecosystem

JigyaSci is a pioneering, mission-driven startup at the forefront of India's research and development evolution. We are architects of a comprehensive digital ecosystem designed to unify the nation's scientific and industrial communities. Our core identity is that of a catalyst and an enabler, committed to breaking down silos and building bridges within the innovation landscape. We are a technology-first company dedicated to creating a synergistic environment where knowledge, opportunity, and capital can converge seamlessly. Our team comprises experts passionate about fostering a culture of inquiry and enterprise, positioning JigyaSci as the foundational platform for India's next generation of innovators.

Connecting Talent, Opportunity, and Capital

Our primary activity is the development and management of an integrated digital platform that serves the entire R&D lifecycle. For students and aspiring researchers, we provide curated access to expert mentors and tangible career opportunities, including internships, project positions, and PhD openings that are often directly aligned with current industry demands. For academia and industry, we actively foster strategic partnerships and collaborations, creating a direct channel for academic research to address real-world industrial challenges. For innovators and deep-tech startups, we facilitate crucial access to capital by connecting them with corporate sponsorships, CSR funds, grants, and a network of investors, supporting them through the critical stages of scouting and due diligence.

The Engine for Change: Our Integrated Digital Platform

Our strategy is executed through our multifaceted digital platform, which acts as a central nervous system for the R&D community. This platform functions as:

An Opportunity Marketplace:

Where students and researchers can discover and apply for vetted positions.

A Collaboration Hub:

A secure space for industries to post challenges and for academic institutions to propose solutions, fostering joint projects.

A Funding Gateway:

Streamlining the process for innovators to connect with diverse funding sources, from grants to venture capital.

An Intelligence Engine:

Delivering data-driven analytics and insights on emerging research trends to help stakeholders make informed decisions.

A Resource Centre:

Curating essential information like research protocols and supporting technology commercialization roadmaps.

Bridging the R&D Chasm: Our Driving Purpose

We are driven by the vision to unlock India's immense, yet fragmented, R&D potential. Currently, a significant chasm exists between academic research and its practical, industrial application. Talented students often struggle to find relevant opportunities, and groundbreaking innovations face immense hurdles in securing funding and achieving commercial viability. The ecosystem operates in isolated pockets, hindering collaborative progress and slowing down the pace of innovation. JigyaSci was founded to address these systemic inefficiencies. Our purpose is to create a cohesive, transparent, and accessible ecosystem where talent is nurtured, collaborations are simplified, and transformative ideas are given the resources they need to flourish and create real-world impact.

Fueling India's Ascent as a Global Innovator

JigyaSci’s work is pivotal to achieving India's ambition of becoming a self-reliant, knowledge-based global powerhouse. By directly aligning with national initiatives like 'Make in India' and 'Startup India,' our platform serves as critical infrastructure for building an Atmanirbhar Bharat. We help curb brain drain by creating compelling research and career opportunities within the country. By fostering a culture of deep-tech innovation and simplifying the path from lab to market, we accelerate the economy's transition from service-driven to innovation-led. Ultimately, JigyaSci is building a self-sustaining pipeline of talent and technology, ensuring India can not only compete but lead on the global scientific stage.
